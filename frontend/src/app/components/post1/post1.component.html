NGO's Helping Hand :
For promoting information sharing , mutual support and awareness among farmers.

Article updated on 19th March 2023

“There are many non-governmental organizations (NGOs) that assist farmers by providing them with various kinds of support, such as training, credit, market linkages, and technical assistance.The NGOs work with farmers in rain-fed and irrigated areas to facilitate the adoption of advanced agricultural practices, including crop production management, soil health management, input-use efficiency, small farm mechanization, horticultural development, ”
-Xiaoying Ren, Director of Strategic Alliance

Here are some examples of NGOs that assist farmers:

  • PRADAN: Professional Assistance for Development Action (PRADAN) is an NGO that works towards the development of marginalized communities, including farmers. They provide training, support, and resources to farmers to help them improve their agricultural productivity, increase their income, and access markets.
  • BAIF Development Research Foundation: BAIF Development Research Foundation is an NGO that works to promote sustainable agriculture and rural development in India. They provide training and resources to farmers to help them adopt sustainable agricultural practices, manage natural resources, and increase their income.
  • Aga Khan Rural Support Programme (AKRSP): AKRSP is an NGO that works to alleviate poverty and improve livelihoods in rural areas of India. They provide training, credit, and market linkages to farmers to help them improve their agricultural productivity and income.Instead of physical money being carried and exchanged in the real world, cryptocurrency payments simply exist as digital entries in online databases describing specific transactions.
  • Self Employed Women's Association (SEWA): SEWA is an NGO that works to empower women by providing them with economic opportunities and support. They provide training and resources to women farmers to help them improve their agricultural productivity and income.
  • Kisan Foundation: Kisan Foundation is an NGO that works towards improving the livelihoods of small and marginal farmers in India. They provide training, credit, and market linkages to farmers to help them improve their income and access to markets.
